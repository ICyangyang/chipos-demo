Leakage power is independent of activity and depends only on cell state:

### 2. Switching Power (Dynamic Power)

**Input Capacitance per MUX2_X1:**
| Pin | Capacitance (fF) |
|-----|-----------------|
| A | 0.946 |
| B | 0.945 |
| S | 1.920 |
| **Total per cell** | **3.81** |

**Calculation:**
```
P_switch = 0.5 × C × V² × f × α
         = 0.5 × 30.5 fF × (1.1V)² × 2 GHz × 0.5
         = 2.31 µW
```

Where:
- C = Total capacitance = 3.81 fF × 8 = 30.5 fF
- V = 1.1 V (supply voltage)
- f = 2 GHz (clock frequency)
- α = 0.5 (toggle rate, assumed)
